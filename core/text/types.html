Abbreviations¶

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll} \def\mathdef2590#1{{}}\mathdef2590{parameter} & \def\mathdef2641#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2641{(}~~\def\mathdef2642#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2642{param}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}^\ast~~\def\mathdef2643#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2643{)} &\equiv& (\def\mathdef2644#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2644{(}~~\def\mathdef2645#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2645{param}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}~~\def\mathdef2646#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2646{)})^\ast \\ \def\mathdef2590#1{{}}\mathdef2590{result} & \def\mathdef2647#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2647{(}~~\def\mathdef2648#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2648{result}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}^\ast~~\def\mathdef2649#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2649{)} &\equiv& (\def\mathdef2650#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2650{(}~~\def\mathdef2651#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2651{result}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}~~\def\mathdef2652#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2652{)})^\ast \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]
